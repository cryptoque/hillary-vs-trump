FAQ

What is this website?

The upcoming presidential election of the United States will affect, not only America, but the entire world! Outside of the USA we are all silently holding our breath, will it be Trump or Hillary? Which candidate do people in other countries prefer? We, Arthur van 't Hoog (design) and Filidor Wiese (development), built this website as a fun side-project in our spare time to answer this intriguing question. Enjoy and please share!

Disclaimer: we are two non-U.S. citizens, who are not affiliated with either political party. By no means is this website intended to be scientifically correct. Please don't take it too serious :)

How does it work?

On this site you can vote for the either Trump or Hillary until the day of the election, November 8th. To discourage possible abuse one vote is allowed per ip-address and anonymous ip's are blocked. The country your vote counts for is determind by the country your ip-address originates from, this can not be changed.

What about my privacy?

We care about your privacy and don't store your ip-address or any other personal identifier in our database. We do store an anonymous (hashed) version of your ip-address to prevent multiple votes per ip.

Which languages are available

We've tried our best to translate most of this site in as many languages as we could to allow for a balanced voting result. Most of these translations are automated and might contain silly errors. Sorry about that.
