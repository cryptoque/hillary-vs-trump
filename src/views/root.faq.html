We the People of the World,

in Order to form a more perfect Society, established this website to give everyone a vote in the upcoming U.S. election.

On November 8th 2016, either Hillary Clinton or Donald J. Trump will be elected as the 45th president of the Unites States. The outcome of this domestic election will have a global effect. People from all over the world are watching the U.S. closely during this process. We follow all the scandals, debates, polls - and wished weâ€™d had a vote.

Now you can!

On this website anyone from any country can cast a vote. Who would Argentine prefer? Or Belgium? Or China? And what about Zimbabwe? The results will be accumulated on our visual result map, which is updated in real time - so check back often!

How it works
  • One vote is allowed per ip-address
  • We care about your privacy and won't store your ip-address or any other personal identifier in our database. We do however store an anonymous version (hashed) of your ip-address to prevent multiple votes per ip
  • We've tried our best to translate most of this site in as many languages as we could. There might still be some silly errors, sorry about that.
